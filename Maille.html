Let's Make some Maille

It's just little round circles of metal than has been industrially 'knitted' together. You can use most any kind of metal wire: Steel, Aluminum, Copper, Silver, Gold, Brass ... pick one and let's knit. Gold and silver make spectacular jewelry. Aluminum will be the easiest to bend. I just wouldn't take it with you into battle ... well unless your are in the band Red Fang.

Making rings is a snap

Moving from A to B is called "Jump Rings". Moving from B to A are links. Make a bunch of each.

Slide four rings on one Jump ring.

Close the jump ring. Congrats, you've made the 4n1 pattern. One ring is always connected by 4 other rings!

Add more to it! Grab 2 rings and one jump ring. Connect the new jumpring to the existing outer rings

Nicely Done!
